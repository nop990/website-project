Cute Critters

Caterpillars

Caterpillars munch away at milkweed until they can make the transformation to butterfly. Then, they begin their extremely valuable life as a pollinator.

Spiny Orb-Weavers

These tiny spiders play an important role in controlling populations of pest insects, like mosquitoes and gnats.

Iridescent Bees

These colorful bees are an astonishingly vibrant variant of the temnosoma bee.

That's no wasp!

That's right! This is actually a moth. It is called a polka dot moth, and it possesses no offensive capability despite its aggressive appearance.
