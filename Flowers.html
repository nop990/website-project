What we grow

Lantanas

Lantanas produce beautiful clusters of multi-colored flowers that are great for attracting monarchs as well as bees.

Milkweed

Milkweed gives Monarchs a place to lay their eggs, and it provides a food source for their caterpillars.

Azalea

Azaleas can grow into shrubs and come in a wide range of colors. Their large, beautiful flowers are great for attracting pollinators.

Tristan Strawberries

Tristan strawberry plants sprout beautiful magenta-pink flowers which eventually develop into ornate, ovular berries.

Crepe Myrtle

Crepe Myrtles come in a wide range of warm colors, blooming 2-3 times a year.

Thornless Blackberry Bush

This blackberry bush stays nice and small, and will provide you with delicious Fall berries without the "catch".
